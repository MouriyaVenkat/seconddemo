Vijay’s ‘Thalapathy 66’ launched in Chennai with a pooja ceremony

Vijay’s 66th film, to be helmed by director Vamshi Paidipally and produced by producer Dil Raju and Shirish of Sri Venkateswara Creations, was launched today with a pooja ceremony in Chennai. The film’s regular shoot has also commenced.

Rashmika Mandanna will be playing the leading lady opposite Vijay in the movie, with other members of the cast yet to be announced.
